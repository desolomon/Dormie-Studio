# Dormie

## What It Is
Dormie is a dorm room design website targeting college students. The core product is **idea boards** — themed design kits that customers purchase and receive as downloadable PDFs. Each PDF contains clickable links to shop the exact items shown in the design.

## Current Website
[hellodormie.com](https://www.hellodormie.com/)

## How It Works
1. Customer browses themed design kits (e.g. "The Georgia", "The Emily", "The Coco")
2. Customer purchases a kit (individually or bundled)
3. Customer receives an instant downloadable PDF
4. The PDF is an interactive idea board with clickable links to buy each item
5. Customer shops the links to furnish/decorate their dorm to that theme

## Business Model
- Sell downloadable design kit PDFs
- Each kit = a curated mood board + shoppable product links
- Bundles (e.g. 3 kits for $19.99) as upsell
- Additional services: custom dorm design, free mood boards, gift cards

## Target Customer
College students furnishing a dorm room for the first time — looking for affordable, cohesive, professionally curated design without the decision fatigue.

## Design Kit Themes (Examples)
- **The Georgia** — Polished, playful, sweetly Southern
- **The Emily** — Elegant, classic, timelessly chic
- **The Coco** — Playful, polished, coastal

## Key Features of the Site
- Shop/browse design kits
- Style quiz to match customers to a theme
- Blog (The Dormie Edit)
- Free dorm essentials checklist
- Custom design services
- Gift cards
